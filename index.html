Why Proactive Safety Matters

Most water damage, fires, and home injuries are preventable with regular safety checks. HomeSafe360 documents risk and catches small issues before they become five-figure problems.

  • Documented inspections for insurance & landlords

How HomeSafe360 Works

1. Baseline Safety Inspection

We start with a full 90-point inspection, photo documentation, and a clear action plan for your home or rental portfolio.

2. Ongoing Check-Ins

Monthly, quarterly, or annual visits to test alarms, scan for leaks, verify ventilation, and re-check key safety points.

3. Digital Reports & Alerts

You receive a clean, simple report with photos, pass/fail items, and recommended upgrades or add-ons.
